Chapter 11, Applications 58-59

  • Use the tool below to explore the orbits of points inside the middle hexagon (the "heart" of the gingerbread man). Describe these orbits.
  • Use the tool to explore the orbits of points inside the other hexagons (the "limbs" of the gingerbread man). Describe these orbits. You might try to prove some of this as well. The dynamics inside the "heart" would be a good place to start.
